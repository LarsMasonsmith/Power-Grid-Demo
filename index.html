Cleaning Up The Grid

Original Carbon Output: 14,525 metric tons per GigaWatt hour


The Goal of this simulator is to rebuild Texas's Power grid to a carbon Output
of less than 3000 tons per GWHR. Nuclear power is always active , whereas
rewnewables are active at certain times of the day. Wind is more active at
night, and solar is more active in the day. Coal and Natural Gas are only active
for 40% and 57% of the day, but that can be at any time.

Capacities:

    Nuclear: 1 GW
    Coal: .3 GW
    Coal-Nuclear: .3 GW
    Natural Gas: .2 GW
    Winmill Plant: .2 GW
    Solar Plant: .05 GW
